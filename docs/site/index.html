00 // Executive Summary

MHRQI is a state-of-the-art quantum framework designed for high-fidelity medical image processing. By combining Magnitude-based registration with Hierarchical decomposition, MHRQI enables efficient encoding and advanced denoising capabilities on near-term quantum hardware.

Statement of the Problem

"Medical imaging, particularly Speckle-corrupted ultrasound and OCT scans, requires precise edge preservation while suppressing Gaussian and Poisson noise. Traditional quantum image representations (FRQI/NEQR) suffer from global dependencyâ€”small local shifts corrupt the entire state. MHRQI solves this by localizing information within a magnitude-hierarchical tree, allowing for localized unitary diffusion that respects structural seams."
