00 // Executive Summary

MHRQI is a quantum framework designed for high-fidelity medical image processing, specifically targeting **Brightness Scan (B-SCAN) Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) Images for Retinal Imaging**. By combining Magnitude-based registration with Hierarchical decomposition, MHRQI enables efficient basis encoding and advanced noise suppression on near-term quantum hardware.

Statement of the Problem

"Medical imaging requires precise edge preservation while suppressing complex noise.
